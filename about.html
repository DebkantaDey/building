About Us

BD Brickwork Limited (IRE) are reputed business specialise in bricklaying with a lot of experience in building houses, extensions, renovation, domestic & commercial works including small to large scale projects. We take pride in our work and make it our top priority to meet deadlines to ensure customer satisfaction.

Working with both private and commercial clients we strive to achieve all expectations that are relayed to us and also to complete all projects in a professional, competent and safe manner.

All our works are finished to high standard and fully insured. We have a wealth of experience in brickwork, stonework, and general building. Our motive is to provide first class service when dealing with clients, always top quality work.
